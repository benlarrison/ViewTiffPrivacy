View Tiff Privacy Policy

At Waterplot, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to ensuring that your data is handled securely and transparently.

Data Collection

View Tiff collects data from your device's camera solely for the purpose of adding effects to your photos and videos. We do not store or access any data beyond what is required to apply these effects during your session.

Ownership

All photos and videos processed through View Tiff are your property. We do not claim ownership or rights to your content. Your media remains entirely yours, and we do not share or use your photos or videos for any other purposes.

Security

View Tiff does not store your camera data or processed media on our servers. All processing is done locally on your device, ensuring that your content stays private and secure.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at bf@waterplot.net.
