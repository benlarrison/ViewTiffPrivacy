View Tiff Privacy Policy

At Waterplot, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to ensuring that your data is handled securely and transparently.

Data Collection

View Tiff collects data from your device's camera solely for the purpose of adding effects to your photos and movies. We do not store or access any data beyond what is required to apply these effects during your session.

View Tiff uses the TrueDepth API exclusively to collect facial data points necessary for enabling real-time camera filters and effects like emojis, glasses, and duck mode. This data is processed entirely on your device and is not stored, transmitted, or shared with any third parties. The facial data remains under the sole ownership of the user and is used solely to provide the intended functionality of the app.

Ownership

All photos and movies processed through View Tiff are your property. We do not claim ownership or rights to your content. Your media remains entirely yours, and we do not share or use your photos or movies for any other purposes.

The face data collected by View Tiff remains entirely on your device and is processed in real time. It is never stored, transmitted, or shared with third parties. You, the user, retain full ownership of your facial data. View Tiff does not save or retain this data in any form, ensuring your privacy and security.

Security

View Tiff does not store your camera data or processed media on our servers. All processing is done locally on your device, ensuring that your content stays private and secure.
